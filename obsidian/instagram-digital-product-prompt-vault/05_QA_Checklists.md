# QA Checklists

## Universal Pre-Publish Checklist
- Audience is specific
- Problem is concrete
- Result is clear
- Offer is believable
- Copy does not invent evidence
- Tone sounds human
- CTA is soft but obvious
- Content is useful even before purchase
- No corporate filler
- No manipulative language
- No fake urgency or scarcity
- Can be posted today with minimal edits

## Product Idea QA
- Is the product easy enough to build quickly?
- Does the buyer understand the result fast?
- Is the format matched to the audience?
- Is the price believable for the market sophistication?
- Is there a natural upsell?

## Caption QA
- Does the hook stop the scroll without lying?
- Is the story short and relevant?
- Are the 3 outcomes practical and buyer-centered?
- Is proof truthful or clearly marked as placeholder?
- Does the CTA feel easy to act on?

## Carousel QA
- Slide 1 creates curiosity or tension
- Slides 2-6 teach one thing well
- Slide 7 busts a real myth
- Slide 8 includes real proof or a clear placeholder
- Slide 9 asks for the next action simply
- Each slide is readable at mobile speed

## Reel QA
- Spoken lines sound natural aloud
- The first 3 seconds create enough interest
- The story moves logically
- The result is specific
- The CTA is clean and easy to say

## DM QA
- Message 1 gives genuine value
- Message 2 builds trust without dragging
- Message 3 invites, not pressures
- Objection handling is respectful
- Silence branch is calm and non-needy

## Optional Master Meta-Prompt

```text
I sell digital products through Instagram and want conversion-focused, human-sounding content that does not feel pushy.

First, identify which of these 7 tasks I need most based on my goal:
1. product idea generation
2. product outline
3. bio writing
4. carousel creation
5. sales caption
6. reel script
7. DM sequence

Then:
- tell me which one to use first and why
- ask for only the missing inputs needed
- generate the deliverable in a ready-to-use format
- keep it commercially strong, warm, specific, and ethical
- do not invent proof, results, or current events

If my inputs are weak, improve them before writing the final output.
```
